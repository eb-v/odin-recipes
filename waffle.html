Classic Waffles Recipe

A waffle that the whole family can enjoy.

The perfect breakfast for a Sunday morning. Both crispy and fluffy, this waffle will make sure that you are delighted. Follow the recipe below so that you too can enjoy the flavor of this waffle.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups warm milk
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
