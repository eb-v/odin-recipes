Classic Waffles Recipe

A waffle that the whole family can enjoy.

The perfect breakfast for a Sunday morning. Both crispy and fluffy, this waffle will make sure that you are delighted. Follow the recipe below so that you too can enjoy the flavor of this waffle.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups warm milk
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter and vanilla. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended.
  3. Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp. Serve immediately.
